# World Tweaker
## Alter the world however you want it

Allows you to alter the following world settings:
- Road length
  - Less than 5,000 km roads won't count towards the Long Driver role
- Straight or normal roads
- Building density
- Object density
  - Small rocks, cacti, foliage, etc
- Large rock density
- Item spawn rate
  - Controls the amount of items that spawn in buildings and buried underground
  - This also works for SgtJoe's Points of Interest mod buildings
- Fluid amount
  - Controls the quantity of fluids that spawn in containers and building taps
- Crate modifier
  - Allows modifying crates to reduce the quantity of items and randomise their conditions
- World type
  - Flat worlds
  - Vanilla world
  - Canyon
    - The road spawns in a canyon deep beneath the surface
  - Road bridge
    - The road is suspended on ground high above the surface
  - Tropical
    - Inspired by RUNDEN's tropical mod for the winter 2019 build
    - Adds water, swimming, buoyancy and palm trees
  - Lava
    - Similar to road bridge, but the world is lava
    - Don't fall in, it might be a little hot

Settings accessible by clicking 'Start' on the main menu\
Cannot be applied to existing saves, except for item settings
